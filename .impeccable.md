## Design Context

### Users
- **Talent**: Black innovators and founders seeking connection and funding.
- **Capital**: Investors looking for "rational capital" opportunities in Black-led startups.
- **Community**: Supporters of equity and innovation in the tech space.

### Brand Personality
- **Refined**: High-quality, polished execution.
- **Minimalist**: Focus on what matters, stripping away the noise.
- **Structural**: Built on a foundation of equity and community.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme**: High-contrast Dark Mode.
- **Typography**: Editorial pairing of 'Antonio' (Display) and 'Quicksand' (Body), with 'Apparel' (Serif) for accents.
- **Visuals**: Grayscale imagery, bold typography, clean lines, and purposeful spacing.

### Design Principles
1. **Refined Minimalism**: Every element must earn its place. Use whitespace as a structural component.
2. **High-Contrast Editorial**: Maintain the "magazine" feel with bold headings and clear hierarchy.
3. **Seamless Mobile Experience**: Navigation should be intuitive (Classic Hamburger) and content should reflow elegantly without losing impact.
4. **Purposeful Motion**: Use subtle transitions to guide the eye without being distracting.
